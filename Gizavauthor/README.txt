This plugins creates a avatar upload option in the author edition page, enhancing the original gravatar tool.
Additionally, it add the possibility to show the avatar of the author of a post by simply adding a widget.

Installation :

Once the plugin uploaded on your website, just click the "Activate" link on the dedicated plugins section and that's it !
Then go of the your profil in the backend section and use the new created upload form at the bottom of the page. Note that you can come back to the original gravatar just by checking the checkbox.

To display the avatar of a post's author, just go to the widget section and drag the GizAvauthor widget into your menu.
